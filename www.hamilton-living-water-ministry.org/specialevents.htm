Special Events

We offer a variety of special events throughout the year

Christmas Shoppe

Each year, the Christmas Shop provides gifts to the children that participate in our programs with the help of many organizations, individuals and churches.

Any organization or church can become a sponsor organization. We need help with funds, purchasing toys, administrative prep work, wrapping presents and much more.

Health Fairs

Periodic Health Fairs provide free medical screening for all who come. Food and drink is provided and there are activities and special treats for the children.

Summer Block Parties/Barbeques

This is an opportunity to get to know people in the community and build relationships. We provide free music, food, games, balloons and lots of activities for the children.

E-Newsletter

You can keep informed about what's going on at Hamilton Living Water Ministry, Inc by signing up for our e-newsletter.
